THE CITY'S VISION

  1. To build up people for Go, for academic excellence, manifesting Christain virtures with hope of making heaven.
  2. To equip and encourage Anglican Students to continue to beeffective in their respectivechurches during their stay on campus and having left campus.
  3. To build people with passion for missions, evangelism during their stay on campus and afterwards.

BRIEF HISTORY OF ASF

Anglican Youth Fellowship University of Ife was formed in 1975. It was the first of its kind. The first goal was just to meet as anglicans but it was quicly redeifned to have the objective of being a revival vehicle for the mother church and achieve academic excellence for its members.

During the early days different fronts of the fellowship in diferent institutions went by different names therefore, a task force was set up to fix these.

The mandate of the task force was to :

  1. Geta common name for the fellowship.
  2. Get a constitution for the fellowship.
  3. Perform the first election and hand over to the first executives at the national level.

Venerable Olu Oshewa sponsored all the meetings and transport fares during the early days.
The name of the fellowship was changed to ASF in the year 1997. Under the leadership of the then Primate Arcbishop Adetiloye.
On march 7 1998 the task force handed over to the new executives at the national level and the draft constitution was accepted and signed.
